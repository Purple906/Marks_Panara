IN THE END, WE ONLY REGRET, THE CHANCES WE DIDN'T TAKE!

Panara Imports opened in 1992 as Marquette's original hippie store with one of a kind clothing and gifts for mind - body - spirit.

After being located in the mall for many years the store returned to its original location in downtown Marquette, where I bought it in 2012.

A huge fan of the store for years, I am working to keep the vibe alive so that people who like the unique and the unusual will be able to visit for many years to come. The stores items have a story to tell, and visiting the store is an experience for the senses.

Carrying on with the traditional Panara Fare I used the first two years to learn the ropes, to become acquainted with the vendors, and to put my stamp on the look and feel of the store. I conduct business with one goal in mind, offer Unique Fashions and fabulous finds while featuring Fair trade merchandise from around the world, eco-friendly and organic clothing and the work of local craftspeople.

Panara Imports is a locally owned store that is committed to supporting local artists and established a consignment program as a way for them to showcase their work.

In addition, I became very interested in the Principles of Fair Trade and to finding products that support families throughout the world by paying fair wages, and not employing children. Product lines that are committed to using sustainable materials, and to providing skills and education for women and children.

I've made some small steps in my goal to develop a store that reflects my values. The outcome should be a store that not only provides high quality merchandise at reasonable prices, but one that also supports and educates about the power we all have to make choices that ultimately make a difference in someone's life.

Panara has specialized in unique finds and fabulous fasions, and has been operating since 1992.
